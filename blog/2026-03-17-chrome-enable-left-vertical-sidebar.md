更新 chrome 浏览器到最新版本

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar01.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar01.webp?raw=true)

在地址栏键入 `chrome://flags` 

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar02.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar02.webp?raw=true)

搜索 `Vertical Tabs` 启用 并重启浏览器

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar03.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar03.webp?raw=true)

**在顶部标签栏空白处鼠标右键：**

点击 `将标签栏移至侧边` 

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar04.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar04.webp?raw=true)

或者在此地址 `chrome://settings/appearance` 

去选择 `标签栏位置` 为 `侧边`

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar05.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar05.webp?raw=true)

就可以了！

![chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar06.webp](https://github.com/xingshi012/blog-images/blob/main/blogs/2026/2026-03-17/chrome-enable-left-vertical-sidebar06.webp?raw=true)

**目前尚且属于测试模式 功能可能不是很完善**
