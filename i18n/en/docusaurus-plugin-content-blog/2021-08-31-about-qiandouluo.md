> An article I wrote several years ago about the game "Contra: Return" seems to be full of emotions now, so I will leave it here intact.
<!-- truncate -->
Qian Douluo, your real name should be Contra: Return, but I like to call you Qian Douluo. You are a game that has witnessed my growth for four years.

When I first started playing the game, I met a scammer who helped me build Dragon Dragon Cannon for free. I believed the scammer and was defrauded of 2,000 yuan. I still remember it in my heart and have never forgotten it. It can be regarded as a lesson to me.

Although you are tired when you go online and do tasks every day, who can understand the joy you feel when you save enough diamonds to draw new heroes?

Although I have had an account and created more than 1,500 days, I haven’t actually played for long. I have only logged in for a year in total. I don’t have any good weapons, and I am not particularly familiar with the group. I can only say that it is about the same. After all, I am the only one who is underage.

I love Qian Douluo. If the game can still exist after graduating from high school, I will definitely take it seriously. After all, it is a game that has witnessed my growth for four years.

I don’t know why I really like this game. Is it because I’m afraid of others saying I’m not good at playing games? I don’t know, but I just like this game. I feel like it would be good if there were ten of them in my school who could play games, or even less.

I remember spending more than 100 yuan to buy diamonds when I played this game in 2019. I remember it was to draw the new hero Frey. Now I think about how ridiculous I was at that time. What really aroused my love for this game was the epidemic in 2020. That epidemic made me explain to a group of like-minded friends who played Qian Douluo on Kuaishou, but There are still people who can still be contacted, probably only @ACE Sasuke is left. Time flies, and the epidemic happened a year ago in the blink of an eye. There are still many things to say, but how many people can really listen. Goodbye, dear Qian Douluo. The next time we see you may be a year later. My studies are my priority, so I retired for a while! See you Qian Douluo in one year!
