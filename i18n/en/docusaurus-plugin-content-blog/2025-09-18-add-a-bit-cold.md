I ended my day's work tonight and shut down my computer. When I walked to the kitchen to prepare for cooking, I felt a cold breeze blowing over my body. I looked at the weather on my phone and saw that the temperature had reached 20 degrees. I was already feeling a little chilly wearing half-sleeves and shorts. It turned out that summer was over and autumn was coming.
<!-- truncate -->
At this time last year, I was still immersed in free time - completing practical training tasks in the school's training base. At that time, it was just ordinary. Now looking back, it was really an unforgettable experience. From Monday to Friday, I was just learning some skills I like (of course, without the constraints of the teacher) Most of the time may be spent "catching fish" (hhh). On Saturdays and Sundays, I am more casual. I usually ride a bicycle to the surrounding markets to buy some fruits, and then go to Jialejia (a large local supermarket chain in Weifang City) in the city or district to buy some bread and milk for breakfast. It is quite a good memory.

A year has passed. After all, I have been working for almost a year. Time flies so fast. This year’s life is like a calm surface of water. It is calm, but there will be ripples from time to time. I look through the photo album on my phone. This year I basically spent walking in the city of Tianjin. Because I work in this city, I have nothing to do on Saturdays and Sundays. I finish my housework. I would go out for a stroll. I was already familiar with the Haihe River. I walked from the Eye of Tianjin to the Jiefang Bridge and felt the beautiful scenery along the Haihe River and the unique bridges.

On New Year's Day, I experienced the New Year's Eve atmosphere, it was crowded, that's all // face covering

I went to see the Five Avenues in spring. Thousands of flowers bloomed and there was a huge flow of people. While admiring the flowers, I also went people-watching hahh~

I went to see the sea in Tianjin. Maybe it is because it is adjacent to the inland sea of Bohai Sea. I feel that the scenery is a little less beautiful than that of Yanweiqing in the province. But if the weather is good, the scenery is also very beautiful.

In addition, the most common things on my mobile phone are photos of my own cooking. I have become proficient in making simple stir-fries. Of course, home-cooked meals may not taste very good. Just focus on eating them // Dog Head

At this point, the action is almost over. I originally wanted to write a high-level article using gorgeous words, but the "dialect" came up when I wrote it. Maybe the more prosaic the language, the more empathy I can create!
