## First of all, it must be an introduction to the concept
AI (Artificial Intelligence), the English abbreviation is AI. It is an important driving force for a new round of scientific and technological revolution and industrial change. It is a new technical science that researches and develops theories, methods, technologies and application systems for simulating, extending and expanding human intelligence.

Artificial intelligence has actually always been a technology around us. Maybe ten years ago, it was little known, or it could only be called by name, and I didn’t know what it did.

But now, everything from voice assistants on mobile phones to sweeping robots to driverless cars are all within the scope of artificial intelligence technology.

## Some application areas:
In agriculture, the application scenarios of smart agriculture include smart agricultural machinery, smart plant protection, smart irrigation, smart breeding, etc. Using smart technology, agricultural production can be automated, intelligent, and refined, improving agricultural production efficiency, reducing costs, improving the quality and safety of agricultural products, and promoting sustainable agricultural development.

In the financial field, we help you analyze a large amount of user data and display the data visually. Of course, this is not just AI but also includes big data technology. Intelligent finance is the application of artificial intelligence technology in the financial industry. Its development has promoted the digitalization and intelligence of the financial industry. AI can be applied in financial risk management, investment decision-making, credit assessment, customer service, etc., improving the efficiency and accuracy of financial institutions. \
For example, AI can analyze large amounts of financial data and market intelligence, predict price fluctuations of stocks, currencies, commodities, bonds, etc., and provide investors with more accurate investment advice and risk control strategies.

Smart homes, sweeping robots, and smart speakers (Xiaodu, Xiaoai Audio) use voice to control the use of lights, curtains, air conditioners, TVs and other furniture in the home.

Smart healthcare is the application of artificial intelligence technology to the medical field, using intelligent technology to solve various problems in the medical field, including medical data analysis, disease diagnosis, drug research and development, health management, etc.

AI is now well known to everyone. I think it is probably because of the popularity of one of its branches, generative AI. What is generative AI?

Generative artificial intelligence (GAI) is an artificial intelligence technology that uses complex algorithms, models and rules to learn from large-scale data sets to create new original content. This technology can create various types of content such as text, pictures, sounds, videos and codes, and comprehensively surpasses the data processing and analysis capabilities of traditional software.

## Future Outlook
The development prospects of AI are bright. Correspondingly, there are many more R&D positions related to AI. Some employment positions have been appropriately added. Life has become more and more convenient. For example, with the popularization of autonomous driving, you can free your hands and go wherever you want comfortably.

But even if there are advantages, there are also disadvantages. The continuous development of AI technology will inevitably cause some traditional positions to disappear. For example, in our programmer industry, with the development of generative AI, some "code farmers" may be eliminated.

## Risks and challenges faced
AI is just a tool. Don’t rely too much on AI. It can easily make people lazy and unwilling to make progress.
Countries can avoid possible risks by using relevant laws and regulations
Using generative AI may cause some data leakage

For example, if practitioners in the financial industry hand over some sensitive data to AI tools, it will cause losses to the company.
