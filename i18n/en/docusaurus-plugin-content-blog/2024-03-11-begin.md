I had nothing to do, so I registered a public account to record the trivial matters in study, work and life. The trivial things were described in simple words, which is the origin of the name of this public account.
<!-- truncate -->
In the age of science and technology, the ways and means of obtaining information have become more and more diverse, and the clutter is becoming more and more fascinating. If you read too much, you will easily be immersed in it, unable to extricate yourself, or lose yourself. With the blessing of big data technology, the video platform's algorithm for recommending videos is calculated in advance. The more you watch, the more videos you like will be recommended to you. If you watch too many videos, you will get bored. I don't like this. I am a person who likes to explore things by myself. If I am interested in a certain topic, I will collect relevant information myself to meet my needs. Watching too many videos will have side effects.

With the continuous development of technology, the time we have to obtain information is continuously reduced. In the era without computers and mobile phones, we may need to read many books to understand a concept. With computers, understanding a concept only requires an article compiled by others, and it takes ten minutes to understand a concept. After the popularity of video platforms, understanding a concept may only require a video of one minute or even dozens of seconds.

It may be influenced by many factors. Recently, I am particularly fond of content that relies on written media. When I get up in the morning, an article on the morning news will help me understand the big and small things that happened yesterday. When I am studying in the classroom, I encounter a learning problem, and the answers to the knowledge are recorded on Baidu. I have nothing to do at night, lying on the bed, or watching videos, or watching what I like. Articles by technical experts, an original article, is a small tool that reflects the author's thoughts. Only by reading it can you understand. This is why I prefer written expressions and the purpose of creating this official account. I hope that I can stick to my original intention and continue to update this official account. This is also a platform to record myself and improve myself.

I am not very talented, my writing style is a bit rusty, I am fond of verbal expression, but my literary talent is not very good.

Thank you for watching, enjoy this
