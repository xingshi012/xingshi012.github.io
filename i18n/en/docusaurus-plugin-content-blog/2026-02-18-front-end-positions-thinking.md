> I just searched [Nanfu International Station Official Website](https://nanfu.global/) and I feel like I have some thoughts about front-end development and my current future career plan. I might write something.

<!-- truncate -->

I have been working for more than a year now, and I have a clearer understanding of my favorite job position. It seems that what I like is not the traditional interface of transmitting data to the backend, and using the UI framework to build a cookie-cutter front-end page of the backend management system.

What I like more is an exquisite website that is interactive and has good aesthetics. I have been involved in the customized development of e-commerce independent websites in my work for more than a year. I have come into contact with many aesthetically pleasing and highly interactive websites. I personally prefer this type of website.

I feel that the website front-end of the backend management platform is a bit boring, but if you want to learn technology, it is estimated that the traditional front-end will improve the technology more. Let’s lay a solid foundation first. Mainstream frameworks such as React Vue are definitely indispensable.

In addition, try to improve yourself, or get closer to the front-end development engineer who focuses on front-end pages, and try to learn some visual front-end technologies such as Canvas, svg animation, threeJS, WebGL, etc., as well as some design-related knowledge that you may come into contact with.

If you are looking for a job in the future, you should still think about finding a position that can build an interesting website. I feel that being able to build an aesthetic online website will give me a full sense of accomplishment.
