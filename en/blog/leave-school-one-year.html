Leave school one year

August 26, 2025
Xingshi
Learn and improve myself.

It’s been a long time since I calmed down and typed an article seriously. Let me sum it up. The past year - from the days when I walked out of campus to now, has passed so fast! A year has passed in the blink of an eye - leaving the hotbed of campus and entering the "shackles" of society. I have experienced some things this year. Although they are not big things, I have also learned some things and gained some knowledge. I have also grown a lot. Let's talk about it in detail :)

In July last year, I officially walked out of campus and started a year of practical training. During the internship phase, first came the practical training phase. The first training unit I found was a bit unpleasant. It was not very satisfactory in Qingdao. I stayed there for a month and said bye.

The days after that I returned to Weifang, the city where I went to university, and spent the rest of my training life in the training base set up by the school. There was no pressure at all. It was almost the same as my life in school. I didn't learn much real things. My daily life was the 'work station' of the training. The dormitory was two o'clock and one line. I have to mention it here. It is really inconvenient to eat here. The place is a bit remote. I remember that the breakfast at that time was bread and milk bought from Jialejia during the weekend break. After several months of training, it seems that the training time was quite fulfilling. From Monday to Friday, I studied by myself. I learned the skills I like. On Saturdays and Sundays, I rode my bicycle to watch the days and went to the surrounding markets. I also spent this time happily.

Time has slowly come to December, and I have arrived in my favorite city. I think I have found the right job position that I like - front-end development. Although the technology used may be relatively basic. There are only three major native components plus the liquid template language, but this job has two weekends and statutory holidays. I can do what I want to do, which is quite good when I think about it. In this way, I survived the internship with my boss and became a full-time employee. I plan to continue to work in Tianjin in the future, but the current technology is definitely not in place. I am thinking about learning about the react framework, and then I want to develop a blog website (I had this idea a long time ago. I struggled with technology before, but now the technology is almost the same. Of course, except for the backend, I suffer from no aesthetics//face covering). So I am still thinking about learning something. Let’s take a look. I plan to use react’s docusaurus to generate a website, and then I will change the style based on it and seize the time to develop it. The most important thing is to record your own learning journey.

At this point, I have reviewed the work-related content of the past year, and of course there is a small episode. When I was looking for a job in Jinan in December, several companies in Jinan took the initiative to contact you. It was said that you met the requirements of our company, and then came for an interview. Is that okay? I also went for an interview. It was all the same routine - training loan. The conversation was pretty good at first, but in the end it was said that your skills are not very good and you need to learn more. I will provide you with a venue, you just go and study, and then I will sign a "training loan" contract for you. When you first entered the society, I didn't make any money, so I took out a loan. Anyway, I wouldn't do such a thing, so I left after the interview.

Of course, I can't just think about work, life is the main theme. During the practical training period in Weifang, I had free time. I went to Zibo to have a small barbecue, climbed Yunmen Mountain in Qingzhou, and went to Jinan to visit various scenic spots. I felt that travel is such a thing. There are so many plans. It may not be as good as walking casually on your own and then encountering a scenic spot by chance. Take it easy. That's it. After work. Although work is a weekend break and a statutory holiday, it seems that time is always short. I don’t have that much free time. I just cook my favorite side dishes if I have nothing to do, and go out for a walk if I have nothing to do. Spend every day freely and happily.

I’ve been chatting a lot, so I’ll just write it here. The main task at this stage is to take care of myself first. Make more money. The most important thing is to make yourself happy!
